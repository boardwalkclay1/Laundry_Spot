Laundry Bubbles connects people who need laundry done with trusted local washers. Request a pickup, and someone nearby handles the whole cycle — from pickup to delivery.

I need laundry done › I want to wash & earn

Laundry Bubbles is a marketplace platform. Independent washers provide the service; payments are processed securely through Stripe.

How it works
For customers
Tell us where you are, when you want pickup, and any notes. A local washer accepts your request.
For washers
Onboard with Stripe, browse available jobs in your area, and accept the ones that fit your schedule.
Payments
Customers pay securely through Stripe. After completing a job, washers receive payouts to their bank account.
Inside the app

Customer dashboard: request pickups, see your job status, and stay in the loop.

Washer dashboard: view nearby jobs, accept requests, and track your earnings.

Payouts: powered by Stripe Connect. You control your own payout account and banking details.

Laundry Bubbles is a marketplace platform. Independent providers perform services; we only provide technology and payment processing.
